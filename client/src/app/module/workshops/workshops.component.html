Empire Medical Training continues to add new topics to present courses and additional medical workshops and CME courses to its curriculum. Many of our courses overlap between Medical Workshops and Regenerative Workshops due to the symmetry of the topics. Because of the overlap of procedures for medical and symmetry with other courses, Empire offers various membership options for attendees either before or after the program.
